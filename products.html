Products Flavor Guide: Color Coding with Cake Sprinkles

We use one size of chocolate molds, so we indicate the flavors by the colorful cake sprinkles.

Explaining the Tables:

  1. "SKU" (or Stock Keeping Unit) is the Product ID.
  2. "Nougat Flavor" is the Product name.
  3. Each chocolate is topped with different "Cake Sprinkles" combinations to indicate the flavor of the nougat.
  4. "Price/each" is for individual chocolates.
  5. If you hover over the graphics of the cake sprinkles, the shapes will be spelled out.

You may choose either Milk Chocolate Or Semi-sweet Dark when you place your order. For example, you might order 10 orange-flavored nougats dipped in a Milk Chocolate coating, plus 10-lemon-flavored nougats dipped in a Semi-sweet Dark Chocolate coating.
